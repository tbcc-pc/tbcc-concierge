Straight answers

Frequently Asked

Why aren't you the cheapest?

Because the cheapest bid usually hides a cut corner that costs you double later. We scope it right, document it, and stand behind it. If lowest price is your only priority, we'll tell you honestly that we're not your fit.

Do you do the work yourselves?

We're a coordination crew. John personally scopes and manages your job; the hands-on work is done by vetted, licensed independent trade partners we've selected and ranked.

Are your contractors employees?

They're independent trade partners — vetted, licensed, insured, and held to our standards and documentation. You get the accountability of one company with the skill of specialized trades.

What if something goes wrong?

You call one person — us. It's documented before and after, so we make it right against the scope we agreed on.

What areas do you serve?

Columbus & Bartholomew County, Edinburgh, Seymour, Hope, Greensburg, North Vernon, and south Indianapolis when the job fits.

What don't you do?

Cosmetic cover-ups that hide a real problem, lowest-bid races, or jobs we can't do to standard. We'll tell you the truth, even when it's not what you hoped.
